NFIT Site Plan

Nigeria Frontend Internship Tracker

Site Name

Name: Nigeria Frontend Internship Tracker (NFIT)

Reason: This name provides immediate clarity for the target demographic. It specifies the location (Nigeria), the niche (Frontend), and the utility (Tracker), making it highly effective for SEO and branding.

Proposed Domain: nfit.ng

Site Purpose

The platform acts as a bridge for aspiring developers. By aggregating verified internship roles and providing a personal tracking dashboard, NFIT eliminates the chaos of the job hunt. It empowers students to focus on skill-building while the platform handles the discovery and organization of opportunities.

Scenarios

  • "I am a student looking for remote-friendly frontend internships in Nigeria that don't require 2+ years of experience."
  • "I have applied to several companies this week; how can I log these applications and set reminders for follow-ups?"
